# PixForge Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-06-09

## Overview

PixForge ("the App") is a local image processing desktop application for Windows.

## Data Collection

**PixForge does not collect any personal data.**

- No images are uploaded to any server.
- No usage analytics are collected.
- No telemetry is sent.
- No network connections are made by the App.

All image processing (format conversion, resizing, optimization, background removal, effects) occurs entirely on your local device using your computer's CPU and GPU.

## Permissions

The App requests access to:

- **File system (read/write):** To open image files you select and save processed output to locations you choose. The App only accesses files you explicitly open or folders you explicitly select.

No other permissions are required or requested.

## Third-Party Services

PixForge does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.
